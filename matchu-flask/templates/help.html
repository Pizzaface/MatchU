MatchU is designed for ease of use and efficiency!

Setting up projects

  • Upon logging in to MatchU as a teacher, you will be greeted with a simple form asking you to create your first project.
  • Once all your projects are created you will need to copy a list of all of your project codes so you can paste them in to an area for students to view.

Managing Groups

  • Once students begin to sign up for projects using the codes you provided, they will be creating and joining groups. As a teacher account, you do not need to do anything further for setup, but you are able to manage groups in certain ways.

    • Create / Alter Groups

  • You are able to manually create a group for students to join

    • Auto-Assign Students

  • If a student chooses to be auto-assigned to a group with the closest matching schedule, they will be entered into a pool of similar users. We suggest the teacher gives a deadline at the start for students to enter if they wish to be auto-assigned.
  • Once all users have signed up, the teacher can click the 'Auto-Assign' button at the top of the page to place all of those students in to groups.

Register for a project

  • Upon logging in to MatchU as a student, you will be greeted with a simple form asking you to submit
    • Project ID (supplied by your teacher)

Join or create a group

  • Once you access your project page you will see a list of available groups
    • The 'Auto-Assign Group' will enter you into a pool of students that will be auto-assigned based on the closest matching schedules that were created during registration.
    • The teacher will set a date to join a group and on that date he will run the auto-assign function that will place any users in the auto-assign group.
